QR Kit — Privacy Policy Last updated: 2026 QR Kit is built privacy-first. 1. WHAT WE COLLECT • Camera frames are processed entirely ON-DEVICE by Google's ML Kit. They are never sent to our servers. • Scanned content (QR/barcode payloads) is stored locally in an encrypted-at-rest Room database on your device. • We do NOT collect personal information, contacts, location, photos, or files. 2. ADVERTISING • We use Google AdMob to display ads outside of the camera screen. AdMob may collect a non-resettable advertising identifier and basic device information for ad personalization, in accordance with its own privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy • In regions covered by GDPR/UK GDPR, we present a Google UMP consent form before any personalized ads are shown. You can change your consent at any time in Settings. 3. PERMISSIONS • CAMERA — required for scanning. Used only while the Scan screen is open. • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES — optional, used only when you tap "Scan from gallery". • INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — used solely to load ads. 4. DATA RETENTION • All scan history and saved QR codes live only on your device. Uninstalling the app removes everything. You can also clear history at any time in Settings. 5. CHILDREN • QR Kit is not directed at children under 13. We comply with the Google Play Families Policy. 6. CONTACT • contact@koflux.tech
