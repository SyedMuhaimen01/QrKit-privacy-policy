QR Kit — Privacy Policy Last updated: May 14, 2026 QR Kit is built with a privacy-first approach. 1. Information We Collect • Camera frames are processed entirely on-device using Google ML Kit. Camera data is never uploaded to our servers. • Scanned QR/barcode content is stored locally on your device using an encrypted Room database. • We do not collect personal information such as contacts, photos, precise location, or files from your device. 2. Advertising • QR Kit uses Google AdMob to display advertisements outside of the camera scanning screen. • AdMob may collect certain information including advertising identifiers, device information, and app interaction data in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy • In regions covered by GDPR or UK GDPR, a Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) consent form is presented before personalized ads are shown. • Users may change their ad consent preferences at any time through the app settings. 3. Permissions • CAMERA — Required for QR and barcode scanning functionality. Used only while the scanning screen is active. • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES — Optional permission used only when the user selects “Scan from Gallery”. • INTERNET and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — Used solely for loading and displaying advertisements. 4. Data Retention • All scan history and generated QR codes are stored locally on the user’s device only. • No scan history is transmitted to external servers. • Users may clear their history at any time within the app settings. • Uninstalling the app removes locally stored app data. 5. Children’s Privacy QR Kit is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and complies with the Google Play Families Policy. 6. Security We take reasonable measures to protect locally stored data from unauthorized access. 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page. 8. Contact If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact: [contact@koflux.tech](mailto:contact@koflux.tech)
